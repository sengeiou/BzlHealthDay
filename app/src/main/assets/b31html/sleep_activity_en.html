Medical examination

If the amplitude of sleep activity is greater than 50, it can be judged as sleep agitation.

Glossary

Refers to unconscious body movements during sleep.

Typical symptoms

Turning over at night, turning more often, sweating.

Improvement plan

Pay attention to the diet and drink a small amount of drink, such as milk or millet porridge before going to bed. Do some preparation before going to bed, such as hot water soaking feet, massage the center of the foot. Sleeping sideways can effectively reduce body movements. Improving the sleeping environment (indoor humidity, temperature) also helps.
