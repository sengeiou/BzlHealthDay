Medical examination

The red area of the chart shows hypoxia abnormality.

Glossary

The duration of the state in which the physiological oxygen deficiency or the tissue oxygen demand is insufficient.

Typical symptoms

Dizziness, weakness, feeling of difficulty breathing.

Treatment

①Direct oxygen inhalation or aerobic exercise, including walking, jogging, deep breathing, and improving the oxygen-absorbing environment.

②Seek medical help if necessary, and take medicine to improve.
